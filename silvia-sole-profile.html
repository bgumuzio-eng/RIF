Silvia Solé

Senior Investment Analyst

Silvia is a Senior Investment Analyst at RIF, bringing significant experience from the alternative investment and infrastructure private equity space. She has spent nearly a year as a Senior Analyst in VC and Private Equity Funds at GVC Gaesco, managing over €200M in Assets Under Management (AUM).

Prior to RIF, Silvia gained two years of valuable experience as an Investment Analyst at Sinia, the Private Equity & Credit Infrastructure arm of BS Capital, where she contributed to the management of a substantial portfolio exceeding €1 Billion in AUM. Her early career also includes an internship in Wealth Management at Creand WM and focusing on public financing at Evalue Innovation.

Academically, Silvia is a high-achiever, holding a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) from IQS Barcelona and the Paris School of Business, graduating in the top 5% of her class. Her expertise is fundamental for analyzing and structuring RIF’s most complex infrastructure-related investments.
