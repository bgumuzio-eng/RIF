FUND CONTROLLER

María is a young finance professional currently serving as a Fund & Accountant Controller at GVC Gaesco in Barcelona, where she works closely with fund control, corporate accounting, and capital markets. She completed her secondary education at Colegio Sagrada Familia Gavá and Loreto Abat Oliba, reinforcing a disciplined and results-oriented professional profile.
