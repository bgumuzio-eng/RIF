Giovanni Lazzeri

CFO

Giovanni serves as the CFO at RIF, bringing over two decades of extensive experience in structured finance, securitization, and working capital solutions from major international banking institutions.

His career includes a significant 12 tenure as Director of Corporate Securitization for Europe at Société Générale, demonstrating deep expertise in complex financial instruments. He also held key roles such as Head of Securitization Italy at ABN AMRO Bank for 2 years and Structured Finance Manager at Mediobanca for seven years. More recently, Giovanni spent five years as Head of Structured Finance at GNE Finance and 2 years in the same role at NPL Management, in addition to 2 years as Director of Working Capital Solutions at DEMIKA Management.

Giovanni holds a Master's in Economics. His extensive background in structuring robust financial deals across various European markets is foundational to RIF's financial integrity and strategic capital management.
