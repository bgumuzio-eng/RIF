Davide Cannarozzi

Managing Partner

Davide Cannarozzi is a Managing Partner at RIF, bringing extensive entrepreneurial and executive leadership experience, particularly within the energy and technology sectors. He spent 10 years as Co-Founder and CEO of GNE Finance, GNE Invest I, and GNE Ventures, successfully building and scaling businesses focused on innovation and growth.

His executive background includes a combined decade in senior finance roles: 7 years as CFO at Enertika and 3 years as CFO at Wattabit, providing him with deep expertise in financial strategy, operations, and corporate governance for energy-related companies. Davide also holds Board Member positions in multiple organizations, leveraging his strategic insight across various industries. Early in his career, he spent five years as a Sales Director at Italian companies.

Davide holds an MBA from Esade Business School and an Industrial Engineering degree from Politecnico di Torino. His blend of technical knowledge, entrepreneurial drive, and financial stewardship is vital for RIF's long-term strategy and portfolio oversight.
