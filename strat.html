About Us

RIF is a regulated FCRE combining institutional fund management and deep sector expertise in climate and infrastructure, powered by GVC Gaesco and GNE Ventures.

01 — Vision

Our Vision

We back hard-tech founders building the next generation of climate infrastructure — assets with enduring utility, defensible economics, and measurable impact.

02 — Principles

Our Principles

We invest in transformative technologies that generate attractive returns while creating environmental value. Through strategic partnerships and long-term vision, we build enduring assets shaping tomorrow's infrastructure.
